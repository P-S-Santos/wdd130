Activities

Here, we've put together a few Clodder-commemorative activities you can partake in, from traveling abroad to global locations, to something closer to home.

Visit the Vissersdorp Monument,

on the Dutch shores where it all began. Feel the gentle sea breeze while you walk the serene northern beaches. You'll find plenty of touristic accommodation nearby, including restaurants offering popular local Clodder-based gastronomy and seaview hotels.

Come to the Mereford museums,

and learn more about the science and history behind Clodder, as well as the brilliant minds behind its research. Join the Clodder Facilities tour for a look behind the scenes, and prepare for an unforgettable, educational journey!

Clodder Con 2023

Join with other Clodder-curious near you in fun Clodder-themed festivities, as well as panels and Q&A forums led by important figures in the Clodder-verse!
